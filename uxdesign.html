UI/ UX Design

Introduction

Welcome to my design folio, where I present my project focused on designing a healthy and sustainable food mobile web app for students. The aim is to empower students to make healthier food choices while contributing to environmental preservation. Through interviews and observations with students, I gained valuable insights into their needs and preferences. This design folio showcases the culmination of my research, ideation, prototyping, and testing phases. It includes inspiration, sketches, prototypes, user testing documentation, and concept development. By incorporating evidence-based insights and drawing from relevant academic sources, I created a comprehensive solution. Explore my design folio to experience the interactive and engaging platform addressing the intersection of healthy eating and environmental sustainability among students. Together, let's work towards a healthier future for ourselves and the planet.

User Research

The user research for the sustainable food app involved conducting interviews and observations, with 6 participants by myself, and additional data was collected by my classmates from over 100 students. The key research questions focused on understanding their diet, food values, cooking habits, awareness of sustainable food, and their perspective on its impact. The research aimed to uncover insights into their challenges, motivations, and needs regarding sustainable food choices.

Key questions

  • What is your diet like?
  • What are your key values when you choose your food? Why are those important to you?
  • Do you often cook at home or dine out?
  • Have you heard of the term sustainable food before?
  • How would sustainable food affect you?
  • Will you improve your diet in the future?

Theme identify

  • Price as the most concerned value when choosing food
  • Inadequate promotion of sustainable food
  • Lack of information on the production of food.
  • Limited time for cooking and shopping
  • Irregular diets due to a busy schedule
  • Students need continuous motivation
  • Lack of awareness of the effects of food consumption on the environment.

Persona

Before creating the app prototype, a persona is developed to represent the target user. In our group, we initially created three personas: Main Persona, Uninterested Persona, and Picky Persona. However, I made a change to my main persona, now named Michelle. Her awareness of the impact of food choices on the environment grew when she started university and joined environmental clubs on campus. Alongside her focus on sustainability, Michelle is also committed to maintaining a healthy lifestyle and enjoys exploring new foods. She is actively seeking sustainable food sources that align with her tight budget.
